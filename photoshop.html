PHOTOSHOP.

A SHOWCASE OF SOME OF MY PHOTOSHOP WORK.

GENERAL INFO.

Over the years I have generated an extensive list of creations within Adobe's Photoshop. This page displays some of my work from the program.

HERO FACESWAP.

Our Unit 1/2 Creative Digital Media class gave us the task of face swapping our face onto on of our heros. So, as any sane person would do, I chose to swap my face with the National Treasure himself, Kenneth Bone. This photo is from the face-swap.

PHOTO REPAIR.

This photo is from a task that required us to repair a heavily damaged photograph using Adobe Photoshop.

COLOURED HELI.

In order to represent the art principle of colour, I used an image of a helicopter that I took at the Airshow in Melbourne. I used a clipping mask with a gradient in order to create the cool effect which is displayed in the image.

INVERTED JET.

This is another image from the Airshow. This time I had to show the principle of line so I used a photoshop filter to apply this cool effect displayed in the image.
